Eriaverse is an elaborated fantasy world in the best genre’s traditions, unifing play-to-earn games, inner marketplace, personal profile and fully integrated DAO in itself.

Eria Landlords is a global economic strategy, which combines all user’s assets in Eriaverse, allowing to buy, manage, and trade them. Eria Landlords’ gameplay has no ultimate goal. It is an open world, where players travel, trade, craft, join guilds and earn.

Eria Everbright is a collectible game, exclusively oriented on mobile devices, in which player collects, brings up, takes care of and spends time with their digital pets. Pet’s rarity and happines is converted into earnings. Eria Everbright will be the first game in the Eriaverse ecosystem. It’s simple concept and mobile device accessibility will become a great ground for our community’s growth.

Eria Legends is a collectible card game, in which player assembles their own unique NFT card deck and battles against other users. Eria Legends contains rating system, weekly in-game events, and online/offline tournaments. All these competitive mods allow players to earn, and the best of them – break into e-sports and make Eria Legends their main source of income.

Eria Tales is an adventure rogue-like card game, in which player assembles their own unique NFT card deck and sets off to explore the world of Eriaverse. Every new adventure in Eria Tales is unique, and a flexible level generation system will allow players to gather in teams and cooperate. The harder the way, which player follows, the more valuable the reward.

Eriaverse is not only a well-developed fantasy world. Eriaverse is thousands of players, investors, and enthusiasts, who firmly believe in the true digital assets’ ownership in a new era of entertainment industry development and are united in a large international community.
Besides games, Eriaverse includes convenient NFT-marketplace, personal profile, DeFi-strategies, as well as the community-driven. Eriaverse’s mission is to acknowledge and reward players’ skills, efforts, and loyalty, as well as to acquaint the majority with the blockchain’s value. Besides that, we put it first to build a convenient games’ ecosystem, in which items, characters, real estate, collectible cards, and pets are bonded to eachother in a single ecosystem — Eriaverse.

The world of Eria is filled with hundreds of different creatures – from proud and intimidating dragons, wyverns, manticores, gryphons, and thestrals to incredibly cute and attractive pegasi, unicorns, lizards, sphinxes and everyone’s favorite cats and dogs. Moreover, every single one of them does not only have its unique characters and preferences, but also possess one of the 10 elements.

Born from a mystical egg, small pet has to go through all stages of growing up, and you – to escort your friend along this way: monitor the health, hygiene, hunger and happiness of your pet. Its element and species will tell you your pet’s needs. For example, electric wyverns need to release energy regularly in mini-games to avoid stress, and ground turtles prefer to eat plenty of food more often than others do.

In return for care and treats, your little digital friend will reward you daily. In addition, the scale of happiness is one of the determining factors for making the greatest profit. To increase this indicator, you need to play mini-games with your pet, feed it regularly and do not forget about its hygiene and health.

Every pet goes through three stages of growing up, every single one of which reveals new mini-games and P2E mechanics. Free and various mini-games will suit people of all, will make Eria Everbright’s gameplay more interesting, and will please your pet, increasing its happiness.

In addition, up to 5% of all income received from the sale of NFT, our DAO will direct to animal welfare funds.

What are ERIA coins for?

Eria Coin is the main token of the Eria Games ecosystem. ERIA is used for the following purposes:
• Stacking and farming;
• Participation in special events;
• DAO

How can I get the Eriaverse ecosystem’s NFT?

There are many ways to become an NFT owner: participate in the NFT auction get an NFT airdrop for social media activity, NFT will also become available on marketplaces after the first NFT auction.

When will the first NFT auction take place and how can I participate in it?

the first NFT auction will take place at the beginning of the 2nd quarter of 2022, a more accurate date will be published on our social networks and on the website of the eria.world. Before you take part in the auction, you need to get into the whitelist, to do this, leave your application in the discord channel #│whitelist

How and when will the airdrops take place?

Up to half of all NFTs will be distributed to the most active community members. News about NFT airdrops and contests will be constantly published in the discord channel #│airdrops.

Does Eria do charity work?

1% of the total ERIA coins’ volume and up to 5% of the income from the NFT sale will be allocated to animal welfare. By voting of the community, 10 charitable foundations/shelters will be selected, where our DAO will send the allocated funds. You can find more information about our charitable activities on our social networks.
